Motor and Battery

The Ather Rizta is powered by a 4.3 kW PMSM Motor. It comes in two models, Rizta S and Rizta Z. The former comes with a 2.9 kWh battery pack while the latter is available with a 3.7 kWh battery pack. The Rizta S and one sub-variant of the Rizta Z get a 2.9 kWh capacity battery pack. The other variant of the Rizta Z gets a 3.7 kWh capacity battery pack. Charging the 3.7 kWh battery to full capacity takes approximately 6.1 hours.

Ather Rizta Range

The Ather Rizta boasts an impressive range. The ARAI claimed range of Ather Rizta is 160 km/charge. The Rizta S and one sub-variant of the Rizta Z have a claimed IDC (Indian Driving Conditions) range of 123 km. The other variant of the Rizta Z has a claimed IDC range of 160 km

Ather Rizta Suspension

The Ather Rizta features a suspension setup consisting of a telescopic fork and a monoshock. This setup ensures a comfortable ride, regardless of the road conditions

Features

The Ather Rizta is packed with advanced features. It has a spacious floorboard and a wide pillion backrest for comfortable seating. It also offers a generous 56L storage space, with 34L in the boot and an additional 22L in the Frunk. The scooter is equipped with AutoHold, Reverse Mode, and SkidControl for enhanced safety. It also features a multi-purpose charger for phones, tablets, and speakers. The Ather Rizta also introduces Halo Bit, a smart module that adds to your rides with pillion intercom, music sharing, and more. It also offers connectivity features like WhatsApp alerts on the dashboard and integrated Alexa functionalities. The Ather Rizta is available in multiple colors, making it a stylish addition to the electric scooter market.
