FAQs

Are Bajaj bikes suitable for beginners?

Bajaj offers a range of bikes suitable for beginners, known for their ease of handling and affordability.

Do Bajaj electric bikes have a good range?

Yes, Bajaj's electric bikes are designed to have competitive ranges, making them practical for daily use.

What sets Bajaj apart from other motorcycle manufacturers?

Bajaj's focus on innovation, affordability, and sustainability makes it stand out in the industry.

Are Bajaj bikes available internationally?

Yes, Bajaj bikes are sold in over 70 countries worldwide.

Is the Bajaj Dominar 400 suitable for long-distance touring?

Yes, the Dominar 400's comfortable ergonomics and strong engine make it an excellent choice for long rides.

What is the fuel efficiency of the Dominar 400?

The Dominar 400 offers decent fuel efficiency, averaging around 30-35 kilometers per liter, depending on riding conditions.

Can I customize my Dominar 400 with accessories?

Yes, Bajaj offers a range of official accessories to customize your Dominar 400 according to your preferences.

How does the Dominar 400 perform in city traffic?

The bike's agile handling and strong low-end torque make it suitable for city commuting, providing a hassle-free experience in traffic.

Is the TVS Raider suitable for beginners?

The TVS Raider is beginner-friendly, thanks to its easy handling and comfortable ergonomics.

What sets the Raider 125 apart from the standard Raider?

The Raider 125 offers higher performance with its 125cc engine, making it ideal for riders who crave more power.

Are these bikes suitable for long-distance rides?

Yes, both the TVS Raider and Raider 125 are designed for long-distance cruising, with excellent fuel efficiency and comfort.

Do these bikes come with a warranty?

Yes, TVS offers a standard warranty on the Raider series, providing peace of mind to riders.

Can I customize the appearance of my TVS Raider?

TVS offers a range of accessories and customization options to personalize your ride.

Are Hero bikes suitable for daily commuting?

Absolutely! Hero bikes, especially the Splendor, are renowned for their suitability for daily commuting due to their reliability and fuel efficiency.

What makes Hero Connect different from other motorcycle technologies?

Hero Connect offers a unique combination of real-time diagnostics, security features, and connectivity, making it a standout in the industry.

Does Hero offer electric bikes in its lineup

Yes, Hero has plans to introduce electric bikes in its lineup as part of its commitment to sustainability and innovation.

Are Hero bikes cost-effective in the long run?

Yes, Hero bikes are known for their cost-effectiveness, both in terms of purchase price and maintenance, making them a wise long-term investment.
