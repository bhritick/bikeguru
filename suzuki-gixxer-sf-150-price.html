Mileage Tips & Advice

Impressive Mileage

Purchasing a motorcycle or scooter involves considering fuel efficiency, commonly known as mileage. Here are some tips to calculate your bike's mileage accurately:

Keep Track

Regularly record the kilometers covered and the amount of fuel consumed. Whether you're a seasoned rider or a beginner, the Raider series caters to all.

Full Tank Method

Fill your tank completely and note the odometer reading. After riding a certain distance, refill the tank and calculate the difference in fuel consumed.

Consistent Riding Conditions:

Perform mileage calculations under similar riding conditions (e.g., city traffic, highway, etc.).

Top 5 Tips to Maximize Fuel Efficiency

Proper Tire Pressure:

Maintain optimal tire pressure for better mileage.

Smooth Riding

Avoid sudden acceleration and harsh braking.

Regular Servicing:

Keep your bike well-maintained with timely service.

Lighten the Load:

Remove unnecessary weight from your bike.

Avoid Idling:

Turn off the engine when stationary for extended periods.
