Royal Enfield

Royal Enfield is a renowned motorcycle manufacturer based in India. The company has a rich history that dates back to 1893 when it was established in Redditch, England. Initially, Royal Enfield produced motorcycles, bicycles, and stationary engines. Royal Enfield gained popularity for its rugged motorcycles and powerful engines. Over the years, it has become an iconic brand known for its classic and vintage-inspired designs. The distinctive thumping sound produced by its engines has also become synonymous with the Royal Enfield brand. In 1955, the production of Royal Enfield motorcycles shifted entirely to India.

The Royal Enfield RE-Hunter-350 is a popular motorcycle known for its timeless design. Powered by a 349.34cc, single-cylinder, air-cooled engine, this offers a balance of torque and horsepower, making it well-suited for both city cruising and long rides. In terms of torque, the bike produces approximately 27 Nm at 4,000 rpm. This torque output provides the bike with good low-end power, allowing it to tackle inclines and ride smoothly at lower speeds. In the horsepower department, the bike generates around 20.2 horsepower (or 20.07 PS) at 6100 rpm. While not a high-performance motorcycle, the hunter-350's power output is adequate for comfortable cruising and relaxed riding experiences.
